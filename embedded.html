LikeFeeder Introduction

LikeFeeder is an easy-to-use RSS reader that allows you to follow your favorite RSS feed, websites, and podcasts in one place.

1, Podcast supported
You can listen your favorite podcast when reading articles.

2, Preloading technology
Next feed items has been loaded ahead, when you are reading the current one. Never waste time waiting for loading.

3, Full content and direct interaction
App shows feed content by a webview. "Jump to see full" is not a problem any more. And you can leave comment to a blog now.

4, Make for china market only
The Feed source store provide hundreds of hand-collected Chinese feed source in 12 categories, and the number is still growing.

Quick Start Guide

1, Click the '+' button in the top-left corner to find more feed source

2, You can follow this tutorial to Make custom feed source

3, Common shortcuts 

        Navigate to next feed        Cmd + Right-Arrow
        Navigate to pvious feed      Cmd + Left-Arrow
